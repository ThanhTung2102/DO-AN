User Manual
To register for services of TK Hospital, you need to create an account to log in to the system. Please make sure you have successfully logged in!
TK Hospital's service includes three different options:
1. Homecare is a home care service with a doctor
2. Virtual doctor is an online health consultation service with a doctor you trust
3. Schedule appointment is a direct medical examination service at the hospital with Basic Service and VIP Service. *VIP service allows you to choose the right doctor and choose a specific examination time.
